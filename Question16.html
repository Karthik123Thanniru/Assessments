There are also Cambodian (Reamker), Indonesian, Filipino, Thai (Ramakien), Lao, Burmese and M alay versions of the tale.[note The Ramayana was an important influence on later Sanskrit poetry and the Hindu life and culture, a nd its main characters were fundamental to the cultural consciousness of a number of nations, bot h Hindu and Buddhist.
